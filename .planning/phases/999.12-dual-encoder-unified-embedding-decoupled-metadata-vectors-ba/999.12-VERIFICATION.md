## Summary

All 10 must-haves verified in codebase. The dual-encoder architecture is fully implemented:

- **Storage layer:** `VecComponentStore` stores raw e_text and e_meta BLOBs keyed by `(entity_id, component)`, decoupled from fused vectors in vec0
- **Fingerprinting:** `meta_checksum` (title+tags) + `chunk_checksum` (body+kind) give independent change signals
- **Fast path:** When only metadata changes, `_index_file_embed` reads cached e_text from VecComponentStore, calls TEI once for e_meta, recomputes e_fused locally — no re-embed of chunk bodies
- **Schema migration:** SCHEMA_VERSION sentinel + wipe-on-deploy for version mismatches
- **Observability:** search_log table captures every query with weights and top results
